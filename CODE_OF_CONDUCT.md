# Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) (Short Version):

- Be respectful and inclusive. No harassment or personal attacks.
- Assume good intent; prefer constructive feedback and collaboration.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or privately contacting the maintainers. Consequences may include warnings or bans from participation.
